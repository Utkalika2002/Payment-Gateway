ABOUT US

ABOUT US:
Established in 2022, Feed the needy’ is a non-profit that is motivated by our faith in God, spreading His unconditional love, regardless of race, wealth, or creed as we minister to those in desperate need in the country.
VISION:
We aim to achieve that no individual should sleep hungry in rural areas in this pandemic situation.

MISSION:
Our mission is to make quality food affordable to all, while stopping perfectly good and delicious meals from being wasted. We do that based on our 3 core values:
1.Help … everyone to have access to quality, convenient meals.
2.Save …our planet by reducing food waste, one of the main causes of climate change.
3.Eat …delicious meals for a great price and a great cause!
